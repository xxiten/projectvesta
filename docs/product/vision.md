# Product Vision (pointer)

Vesta is a modern, modular hotel property-management platform (PMS), SaaS-ready,
South-Tyrol-first but internationalisable by design. It wins on speed in the
daily operative workflow, first-class integrations, and a calm, keyboard-first,
"Apple-like" UX — not on feature mass.

The authoritative, detailed product & system articulation (vision, personas,
jobs-to-be-done, domain/context map, ER model, UX, roadmap, iteration plans)
lives in the approved plan:

- `~/.claude/plans/du-bist-mein-lead-noble-shell.md`
- Architecture decisions: [`docs/adr/`](../adr/README.md)
- Ubiquitous language: [`docs/domain/ubiquitous-language.md`](../domain/ubiquitous-language.md)

full JTBD and the MVP/roadmap split (R1 operatives Fundament → R2 Integration &
